# AMG Subscription Program Rules

## Public Pricing Position

The public website must not show invented dollar prices, internal crew rates, margins, administrative cost assumptions, profit targets, risk buffers, or internal pricing formulas.

Use:

- `Request Tailored Proposal`
- `Pricing based on aircraft class, crew requirements, selected allowances, and operating scope`

## Standard Aircraft Classes

- Single-Engine Piston
- Multi-Engine Piston
- Turboprop
- Single-Engine Jet / VLJ
- Light Jet
- Midsize Jet
- Large-Cabin / Heavy Jet

## Custom Proposal Only

- Super-Midsize Jet
- Helicopter
- Unusual, experimental, special-mission, or nonstandard aircraft

## Tier Structure

Piston classes use Basic, Core, Priority, and Premier.

Turboprop, VLJ, light jet, midsize jet, and heavy jet classes use Basic, Core, and Priority.

## Included Events

One included Client Flight means one crew duty day. Multiple legs during the same duty day remain one included event. A trip exceeding 12 hours may consume an additional duty day. Overnight activity may consume another duty day depending on duty period and continued assignment.

One included MX Flight means one maintenance-related aircraft movement. Base to MRO is one movement. MRO to base is one movement. A round trip is two movements.

Two-pilot aircraft affect proposal pricing, lodging, travel, and crew-labor assumptions, but internal calculations are not published.

## Allowances

Basic monthly: 2 client-flight duty days and 1 MX movement.

Basic annual: 28 client-flight duty days and 5 MX movements.

Core monthly: 3 client-flight duty days and 1 MX movement.

Core annual: 36 client-flight duty days and 7 MX movements.

Priority monthly: 5 client-flight duty days and 2 MX movements.

Priority annual: 60 client-flight duty days and 12 MX movements.

Premier monthly, piston only: 7 client-flight duty days and 3 MX movements.

Premier annual, piston only: 90 client-flight duty days and 15 MX movements.

## Credit Policy

Unused eligible credits remain available while the subscription is active and in good standing. Credits expire upon cancellation, termination, non-renewal, or account default. Credits have no cash value and cannot be refunded or transferred. Monthly clients may not use future monthly allowances early. Annual clients may use their annual allowance according to annual program rules.

## Annual Billing

Annual subscriptions receive a 10% reduction on eligible AMG administrative and coordination fees only. The discount does not apply to crew compensation, crew travel, lodging, pass-through expenses, vendor charges, airport expenses, or aircraft expenses.

## Exclusions

Owner/operator expenses include fuel, oil, aircraft insurance, maintenance invoices, parts, mechanic labor, aircraft subscriptions, and databases.

Pass-through items include landing, ramp, parking, hangar, FBO handling, deicing, catering, aircraft cleaning, customs, immigration, international handlers, and overflight permits.

Separate-quote items include flight attendant, dedicated dispatcher, full aircraft-management accounting, specialized maintenance representation, international permit programs, AOG support, ferry permits, special flight permits, and unusual-risk missions.
