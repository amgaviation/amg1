# Compliance QA Checklist

- Footer links route to Privacy Policy, Privacy Choices, Cookie Policy, Terms, Mission Acceptance, Credential Submission, Accessibility, and the legal index.
- `/legal` lists all legal notices from the registry.
- `/privacy-choices` submits through a server action and returns a safe user-facing error if storage fails.
- Cookie banner appears on first visit and does not block necessary site use.
- Optional scripts load only after consent category enablement.
- Contact and support forms include required acknowledgement plus optional email and SMS consent.
- Confirmation and internal form emails include submitted fields only, not irrelevant empty fields.
- Portal upload flows show document upload terms before upload controls.
- Admin compliance page is linked in admin navigation and settings.
- Quote defaults do not describe charter contracts or instant acceptance.
- Public aviation copy does not imply instant acceptance, direct charter sale, direct payment, or guaranteed aircraft/crew availability.
