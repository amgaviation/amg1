- Legal entity: AMG Aviation Group LLC.
- Public brand: AMG Aviation.
- Portal name: AMG Aviation Portal.
- Primary contact: `information@amgaviationgroup.com`.
- Public description: professional aviation support and coordination services.
- Positioning line: aviation support built around operational clarity, trusted relationships, and disciplined execution.

Use `AMG Aviation` for public-facing copy. Use `AMG Aviation Group LLC` for legal, contractual, compliance, invoicing, privacy, terms, and regulatory references.

## Service Model

AMG Aviation receives requests, reviews requirements, evaluates operational fit, coordinates appropriate resources, and confirms approved services through established workflows.

Every request is reviewed before acceptance. Submission does not guarantee service, availability, approval, crew assignment, aircraft movement authorization, vendor participation, or final acceptance.

Approved workflow language:

- Submit Request
- Review Request
- Evaluate Requirements
- Determine Outcome
- Communicate Status

Approved billing flow:

- Service review
- Quote or billing determination
- Client approval if required
- Invoice issued
- Payment processed according to approved terms

## Required Disclaimer

AMG Aviation Group LLC coordinates and facilitates aviation-related services. Service availability, crew availability, vendor participation, operational acceptance, and final approvals are subject to review and confirmation.

## Approved Terms

Use terms such as Request, Review, Under Review, Approved, Declined, Quote, Invoice, Crew Coordination, Vendor Coordination, Aviation Support, Aircraft Owner, Aircraft Operator, Client, Crew Member, Vendor, and Portal.

Avoid terms that imply guarantees or instant final outcomes, including Guaranteed, Instant Approval, Immediate Acceptance, Reserve Aircraft, Book Aircraft, Unlimited Support, Automatic Approval, Confirmed Instantly, Emergency Response Guaranteed, Operated By AMG Aviation unless legally verified, and Aircraft Fleet unless owned and documented.
