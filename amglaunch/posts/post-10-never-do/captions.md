## LinkedIn

In aviation, what a company refuses to do tells you more than its sales page. Three things AMG never does:

1. We never supply the aircraft. We are a crew-sourcing and coordination service — not an air carrier. We don't sell flight time, and we never bundle aircraft with crew.

2. We never take operational control. Owners retain it at all times. Go/no-go decisions belong to the owner and the pilot in command — that line never blurs, and your insurance depends on it staying that way.

3. We never mark up pass-through costs. Pilot day rate, travel, lodging, per diem — at cost, with receipts. Our margin lives entirely in flat, published coordination fees and plan retainers.

These aren't disclaimers. They're the design.
