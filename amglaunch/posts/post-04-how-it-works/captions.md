## LinkedIn

Here's exactly what happens after you submit a request to AMG:

Step 1 — Submit (about 5 minutes). Aircraft, tail number, mission type, dates, origin/destination, insurance carrier.

Step 2 — Quote (within 24 business hours; 12 on Standard, 4 on Priority). Itemized: pilot day rate, travel, per diem, and our flat coordination fee.

Step 3 — Crew confirmed (target 48 hours; 24 with first-call priority). Vetted pilots — certificates, medicals, type experience, insurance history, references all verified before you see the name.

Step 4 — Fly. Mission tracked in AMG Connect, with documents, invoice, and receipts at closeout.

The insurance gate matters: no mission proceeds until your selected pilot is named or approved on your policy. SLA tracking starts the moment your request hits our system — the timestamps hold us to it.
