# amglaunch — First 10 Social Posts

Launch content for AMG Aviation Group: Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

## Structure

- `content-calendar.md` — posting order, schedule, and sequencing logic
- `posts/post-XX-*/captions.md` — captions for all 3 platforms + alt text
- `posts/post-XX-*/image.png` — final 1080×1080 artwork

## Images

- Posts 02, 03, 04, 08, 10: branded graphics (Montserrat/Space Grotesk, AMG navy `#050B14`, accent blue `#3B82F6`) — final, in place.
- Posts 01, 05, 06, 07, 09: AI photos generated with Higgsfield (nano banana 2, 1024×1024). If `image.png` is missing in those folders, download from the link in that folder's `image-source.md` (also visible in the Claude chat) and save it as `image.png`.

## Brand guardrails applied

- Voice: direct, numerate, commitment-forward. No banned vocabulary ("solutions" as a noun, "elevated," tier adjectives, etc.).
- Numbers shown everywhere: 24/12/4-hr quotes, 7-day pilot pay, $0 markup, worked example (~$1,210), full plan pricing, day-rate ranges.
- Compliance: never claims AMG supplies aircraft, operates aircraft, or takes operational control; aircraft and crew never bundled; credentials via secure portal only.
- CTAs point to amgaviationgroup.com pages (/pricing, /how-it-works, /pilots, /for-shops, /request).

## Notes

- All images are 1:1 (1080×1080 graphics, 1024×1024 photos) — native for Instagram/Facebook and fine on LinkedIn.
- AI imagery is illustrative. Once real mission photos and team photos exist, prefer them — real people and real aircraft outperform stock-style imagery, and the brand spec calls for visible humans.
- Alt text is included in each captions.md — paste it into each platform's accessibility field.
