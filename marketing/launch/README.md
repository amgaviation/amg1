# AMG Aviation Group — Launch Marketing Campaign

**"Coordinated."** — the public launch of AMG Aviation Group and AMG Connect.
Launch day **Tuesday, July 28, 2026**; a short tease → launch → two weeks of momentum
across **LinkedIn, Instagram, and Email.**

This folder is a complete, ready-to-run campaign: strategy, a day-by-day calendar with
every caption, four emails, and **17 finished, print-ready social creatives** (real copy and
data designed into on-brand graphics — not just stock photos).

## The campaign in one screen

- **Idea:** *Coordinated.* — Crew. Movement. Maintenance.
- **Proof points:** owner-controlled (you keep operating authority) · **$295 flat, zero markup, receipts included** · credential-reviewed crew · quoted in ~12h · one portal (AMG Connect).
- **Arc:** Jul 24 tease → **Jul 28 launch** (LinkedIn hero, IG 5-slide carousel + Reel, launch email) → Week 1 pillars (pricing, crew, ferry, AMG Connect) → Week 2 conversion (maintenance, partners, owner-controlled, closing).
- **Primary CTA:** Start a support request → `amgaviationgroup.com/request-support`.

## How to use it

1. Skim `00-strategy.md` to align on positioning and the guardrails.
2. Load `images/` into your scheduler and schedule each post per `01-content-calendar.md`
   (copy the captions verbatim; they're written to the AMG voice and compliance rules).
3. Queue the four emails from `02-emails.md`.
4. To tweak any creative's copy or design, edit `build/gen.mjs` / `build/kit.css` and
   re-run (see `03-creative-index.md`).

> All dates anchor to a **Tuesday, July 28** launch. If the date moves, shift every entry by
> the same offset — the rhythm (short tease → Tuesday launch → two weeks of momentum) is
> what matters.
