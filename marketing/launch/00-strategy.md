## 1. The brand, in one paragraph

AMG Aviation Group is **owner-controlled aviation support coordination** for
Part 91 aircraft owners, small flight departments, maintenance facilities, and
professional crew. AMG coordinates the people, planning, and paperwork around an
aircraft — **crew coverage, ferry & repositioning, and maintenance flight
support** — while the owner/operator keeps operating authority and final
acceptance. Everything runs through **AMG Connect**, a role-based portal that
keeps requests, documents, quotes, and status in one view. US-based, worldwide
coordination.

## 2. The big idea

**"Coordinated."**

One word that carries the whole promise: the jet, the crew, the maintenance
ferry, the paperwork, the visibility — handled, in sequence, by people who do
this for a living, without taking the controls out of the owner's hands.

Supporting line (the hero eyebrow): **Crew. Movement. Maintenance.**

The campaign leads with calm confidence, not hype. Every asset should feel like
it belongs to an operations desk at blue hour: precise, premium, quiet.

## 5. Voice & guardrails

**Voice:** measured, precise, premium, discreet. Short sentences. Operational
nouns. Confidence through restraint. Never breathless, never salesy.

**Compliance guardrails — do not cross:**
- AMG **coordinates**; AMG does **not** operate the aircraft or hold operating
  authority. Never imply we fly the aircraft or act as an operator/charter.
- The **owner/operator retains operating authority and final acceptance** on
  every request. Say so when relevant.
- Coverage and availability indicators **do not guarantee** immediate
  assignment. Avoid absolute guarantees ("always," "instant," "guaranteed").
- Pricing claims ($295 flat, zero markup, receipts) mirror the site's worked
  example — keep them exactly as stated; don't invent numbers.
- No real client names, tail numbers, or itineraries. Examples are illustrative
  (e.g., "SR22, Tampa → Atlanta").

## 6. Visual system

- **Palette:** midnight navy `#050B14` / `#07111F` · champagne-gold accent
  `#D4AF37` · light canvas `#FFFFFF` / `#F3F4F6` · chrome text `#E8EEF7`.
- **Photography:** editorial aviation at blue hour / dawn — ramps, cockpits,
  hangars, professional crew, ground coordination. Cinematic, high dynamic
  range, subtle grain. Wide negative space for a short headline.
- **Type/overlay:** headline in the site's display face; a mono eyebrow (e.g.
  `01 — COORDINATED`) in champagne-gold; keep text to ≤6 words on-image.
- **Logo:** use `logo-white.png` on navy imagery, `logo-navy.png` on light.
- **Do not** bake long copy or fake UI into generated images; overlay real text
  in the post template so it stays crisp and truthful.
